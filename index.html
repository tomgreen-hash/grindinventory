— SKUs
● Pink dot on SKU — this exact SKU is fulfilled by more than one manufacturer (e.g. COLDBREW-FW by both Inovocan and Muensterland; SACHET-GRIND-BL by both HACO and WE-PACK). Case dims, barcodes and lead times may differ. Click the row for a full comparison.
SL SL on barcode — this RTD can has two separate unit barcodes: one for the sleeved variant (paper/plastic label) and one for the printed variant (design on the metal). Same contents, different barcode at POS and in logistics. Click the row to see both.
BULK Amber row — Bulk Supply (Muensterland) — these case codes and quantities apply only when ordering direct from Muensterland. Stock arrives in 12-packs and is repacked to standard Grind 8-packs before distribution under the regular SKU.
£ Green ALC — Average Landed Cost from Feb 2026 data. Amber means the value has been manually overridden in this session. 60 SKUs have no ALC on file (shown as —).
✎ Pencil on product name — this product has locally-saved edits in your browser. All edits export in the CSV.
